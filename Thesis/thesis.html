Here you can find the code used for the M.Ss. Thesis "Analyzing the interplay between exploration and exploitation in human mobility and social networks" written by Stefanos Marios Chourdakis (s151331) .
This M.Sc. thesis was prepared at the department of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) in the period from January to June 2017. It corresponds to 30 ECTS credits and it was part of the requirements for acquiring a M.Sc. degree in Digital Media Engineering. The project was supervised by Sune Lehmann (Associate Professor, DTU, DTU Compute) and was conducted in Copenhagen, Denmark.


The code is split in parts, following the structure of the Thesis text. Each of the following links will transfer you to the corresponding Jupyter Notebook with the Python code implementation.

  • Human Mobility: 1st Part of the thesis. Chapters 4 and 5.
  • Social Networks Part 1: Call Network - A Search for Dichotomy and Network Studies. Chapters 6, 7, 10 and 11.1
  • Social Networks Part 1: SMS Network - A Search for Dichotomy and Network Studies. Chapters 6, 7, 10 and 11.1
  • Social Networks Part 2: Individual Strategies for Human Interaction. Chapters 8, 9 and 11.2
